What are cookies?

Cookies are small data files placed on your device when you visit a website or use online services. They are widely used to make services work efficiently, enhance user experience, and provide reporting information.

Why do we use cookies?

  • Strictly necessary cookies – required for basic functionality.
  • Analytics and performance cookies – help us understand usage and improve services.
  • Advertising and targeting cookies – where applicable and permitted by law.

Non-essential cookies are used only with your consent, where required by applicable law.

How can I control cookies?

You can manage cookie preferences through browser settings or, where available, our Cookie Consent Manager. Disabling cookies may affect functionality.

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have additional rights regarding cookies and similar technologies. Please refer to our Privacy Policy for more information.
